A vector field, with three spacial dimensions and one time dimension, will be expressed as a bold face script symbol. $$\tv{A}=\tv{A}(x,y,z;t)$$ A script symbol indicates a general time dependence and the bold face indicates the quantity is a vector. $$ \tv{A}=\t{A}_x(x,y,z;t)\uv{x}+\t{A}_y(x,y,z;t)\uv{y}+\t{A}_z(x,y,z;t)\uv{z} $$ The small hat on top of a bold face symbol indicates a unit vector. For example the unit basis vectors in the cartesian coordinate system: $$ \uv{x}=\begin{bmatrix}1\\0\\0\end{bmatrix}\quad \uv{y}=\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\\0\end{bmatrix}\quad \uv{z}=\begin{bmatrix}0\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}\quad $$ \begin{align} \nabla \times \tv{E} &= -\tv{M}_i - \frac{\partial \tv{B}}{\partial t} \\ \nabla \times \tv{H} &= \tv{J}_i + \tv{J}_c + \frac{\partial \tv{D}}{\partial t} \\ \nabla \cdot \tv{D} &= \t{q}_{\text{ev}} \\ \nabla \cdot \tv{B} &= \t{q}_{\text{mv}} \end{align}
